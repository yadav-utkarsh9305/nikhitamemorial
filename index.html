About Nikhita Memorial

Nikhita Memorial (a Unit of Nikhita MEDICARE LTD), one of Varanasi’s best Super Speciality Institute that redefines standards of excellence in healthcare delivery by bringing together the best of infrastructure, technology, training, education and medical intelligentsia.

Nikhita memorial is a 450 bedded Super Speciality Hospital in Varanasi providing all kinds of Medical, Surgical & Diagnostic services to the patients of Eastern UP, Bihar, Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh and MP for more than 20 years. We provide best services in one roof like Cardiology, Nephrology, Medicine, General Surgery, Neurology, Obs & Gynecology, Urology, Oncology, Pediatric, Orthopedic, ENT, Dental department.

Three-day mega surgery camp for laser, laparoscopy, stomach

A three-day mega surgery camp for laser, laparoscopy, stomach, and liver related surgeries is all set to take place from May 13th to May 15th. The camp is organized by a Popular Hospital in the Varanasi city and aims to provide high-quality surgical services to patients at affordable prices. During this mega surgery camp, patients from western and eastern Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Jharkhand, and other neighboring states can avail themselves of a 20% discount on surgery and all tests
